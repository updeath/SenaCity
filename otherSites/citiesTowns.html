Explore the Surroundings

Discover charming places just minutes away from Pereira

Salento

40 km from Pereira

A colorful town known for its colonial architecture and the Cocora Valley.

Salento

  • Cocora Valley - The tallest wax palms in the world
  • Calle Real - Colonial architecture and handicrafts
  • Viewpoints with spectacular vistas
  • Traditional Quindío cuisine
How to get there: Direct buses from Pereira's Terminal

Santa Rosa de Cabal

15 km from Pereira

Famous for its hot springs and traditional "chorizo santarrosano."

Santa Rosa de Cabal

  • Natural hot springs surrounded by nature
  • Local cuisine and "chorizo santarrosano"
  • Parque de las Araucarias
  • Minor Basilica
How to get there: Buses every 20 minutes from Pereira

Filandia

35 km from Pereira

A traditional town with stunning views and typical Quindío architecture.

Filandia

  • Illuminated Hill Viewpoint
  • Main Square and its traditional cafés
  • Handicrafts made of wicker and bamboo
  • Hiking trails
How to get there: Buses from Pereira's Terminal

Marsella

30 km from Pereira

A heritage town with traditional architecture and a botanical garden.

Marsella

  • Alejandro Humboldt Botanical Garden
  • House of Culture
  • Viewpoint overlooking the valley
  • Preserved colonial architecture
How to get there: Regular buses from Pereira's Terminal
